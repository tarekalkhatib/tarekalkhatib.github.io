User Research

The first step was identifying who my main users were through primary and secondary user research, creating user personas, problem statements, and user journey maps. Available on demand.

Experimented with Paper Wireframes, Digital Wireframes, Low-Fidelity Wireframes. Usability studies are the next step in this phase of the project.

Refining the Design

This phase of the project, I will create mockups based on my usability study findings, continue onto the High-Fidelity prototype, and refine Accessibility features.

Next steps
